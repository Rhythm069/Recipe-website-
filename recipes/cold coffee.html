Cold Coffee

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Cold Milk
  • 2 tsp Coffee Powder
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • Ice Cubes
  • Ice Cream (Optional)

Steps

  1. Add milk, coffee, and sugar in blender.
  2. Blend well.
  3. Add ice cubes.
  4. Pour into glass.
  5. Serve chilled.
