Masala Tea (Chiya)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Water
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 2 tsp Tea Leaves
  • 2–3 Cardamom Pods (crushed)
  • 1 small piece Ginger (crushed)
  • 1 small Cinnamon Stick
  • 2–3 Cloves
  • Sugar to taste

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pan.
  2. Add crushed ginger, cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves.
  3. Let it boil for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Add tea leaves and boil for another 2 minutes.
  5. Add milk and sugar.
  6. Boil for 3–4 minutes until rich color appears.
  7. Strain into a cup and serve hot.

⏱️ Time: 10 Minutes | 🔥 Difficulty: Easy
