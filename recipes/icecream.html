Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Fresh Cream
  • 1 cup Condensed Milk
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Essence

Steps

  1. Whip the fresh cream until fluffy.
  2. Add condensed milk and vanilla.
  3. Mix gently.
  4. Freeze for 6 hours.
  5. Scoop and serve cold.
